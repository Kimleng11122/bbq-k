FROM ONE GRILL TO A CITY

BBQ-K started with a belief: Korean BBQ done right — over live fire, with no shortcuts — creates moments people remember. That belief didn't change as we grew. It scaled.

Each new location was chosen deliberately. Each menu was tested against the same standard. We didn't chase expansion — we earned it through guests who kept coming back.

TWO WAYS TO DINE

Our growth revealed something important: not every guest wants the same experience. Some come for unlimited rounds and celebration energy. Others want curated sets and a slower pace.

So we built two distinct paths — All You Can Eat at three branches, Order by Order at two — without splitting the soul of what makes BBQ-K, BBQ-K.
