Our Journey

How BBQ-K Evolved

A story of deliberate growth — from one family's grill to five destinations, without ever losing what made the first flame special.

BBQ-K was never built to be a chain. It was built to be a place where Korean BBQ is done properly — and that standard became the reason we grew.

"The fire doesn't change. Only the table gets bigger."

Today, guests choose between the energy of All You Can Eat and the refinement of Order by Order — two experiences, one unwavering commitment to quality.

2018

The First Flame

BBQ-K opens with one location and one mission: authentic Korean BBQ over live fire — premium cuts, traditional marinades, and the warmth of shared dining.

2020

A Community Forms

Regulars return. Word spreads. The team refines every detail — from banchan prepared fresh daily to the rhythm of service around the grill.

2022

All You Can Eat Arrives

Two new locations launch the unlimited experience — rounds of meat, sides, and celebration. BBQ-K becomes the destination for group dinners and big nights.

2023

The Refined Path

Premium Order by Order locations open with curated set menus from AU$88 to AU$350 — for guests who savour choice, pacing, and precision.

2025

Five Locations, One Soul

Three All You Can Eat branches. Two Order by Order destinations. One brand defined by fire, flavour, and the people gathered around it.

What We Stand For

Our Values

The principles behind every cut, every flame, and every guest who pulls up a chair at our grill.

I

Fire & Authenticity

Real charcoal and traditional technique. We honour the flame because it is where Korean BBQ begins.

II

Quality Without Compromise

Premium cuts and fresh banchan, every day. Our standard is non-negotiable — at every location.

III

Gathering & Community

The grill is a shared ritual. We design spaces for connection — friends, families, first-timers who become regulars.

IV

Warm Hospitality

Attentive without being intrusive. Genuine without being performative. Every guest feels welcomed.

V

Thoughtful Growth

We expand with intention. Each new location earns its place — never at the expense of what defines us.

VI

Tradition & Modernity

Korean roots, Melbourne present. Respecting heritage while crafting an experience for how we dine today.
